Delivered an invited research talk on scalable data pipelines for acquisition, curation, and synthetic data generation for Indic Large Language Models. 

* Discussed imbalances between typical pretraining data scales and alignment data requirements.
* Detailed the imperative for generating high-quality, high-EQ (Emotional Quotient) synthetic datasets.
* Addressed an audience of professors and graduate researchers in mathematical sciences and machine learning.
