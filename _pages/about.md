### 🧬 Research Vision

My research interests lie at the intersection of:
* **Explainable and Bio-inspired AI**
* **Cognitive Foundations of Intelligence**
* **Data and Energy Efficient Machine Learning**
* **Interpretible and Robust Deep Learning Models**
* **Biologically Inspired Representation Learning and Network Pruning**

My overarching long-term research goal is to understand how biological systems process complex information under severe environmental and energy constraints. I aim to leverage these foundational principles to build transparent, data-efficient, and structurally optimized machine learning architectures that bridge the critical gap between empirical black-box deep learning and human-like cognitive intelligence.
